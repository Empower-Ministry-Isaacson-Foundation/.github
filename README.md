## 📖 About

Empower Ministry exists to support Christian leaders by sharing best practices, providing resources, and coming alongside high-growth churches — all at no cost.

---

## 📂 About This GitHub

This GitHub organization is a temporary home for resources shared with our **Empower Ministry Leadership (EML) network** while the official [Resource Center](https://www.empowerministry.com/resource-center) is under development.

Repositories here are **private** and accessible only to invited EML members. Files may change, move, or be retired as the Resource Center comes online. If you're an EML member and need access, please reach out through your usual Empower Ministry contact.
